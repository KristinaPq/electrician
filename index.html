How to save electricity?

How to save electricity with proper lighting

In total, the cost of energy resources for lighting is up to 15% in utility bills. These costs can be reduced by half if the lighting system in the house is properly organized. To do this, it is recommended to combine general, local and combined lighting. To reduce energy consumption, you need to use natural light to the maximum. When leaving the room, do not forget about the switches.

Energy-saving lamps

To save electricity, you can notice incandescent lamps on LED. And even though they are not cheap, it's still worth spending once. In autumn and winter, the lights in the houses are lit for at least 10 hours a day. In summer, about 2-3 hours in the evenings. Working in this mode, energy-saving light bulbs will recoup their price in 5-6 months (taking into account their service life of 3000 hours).
